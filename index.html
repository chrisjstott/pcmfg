Overview

Founded in 1929, the Port Clinton Manufacturing company has long earned and enjoyed the respect of its customers.

That reputation has been built on high quality, close tolerance work.

Port Clinton Manufacturing Co. has long provided its customers with a full range of in-house machining capabilities. This broad range of machining, as well as the sizable capacity of their plant, sets Port Clinton Manufacturing Co. apart from its competitors. With 68,000 square feet of manufacturing capacity and an additional 10,000 square feet of warehousing, Port Clinton Manufacturing Co. can support the most demanding Just-In-Time requirements its customers may have.

As a closely held family corporation, Port Clinton Manufacturing Company prides itself on its owners willingness to give their personal attention to each customers needs.

For further information on how Port Clinton Manufacturing Co. can increase your profitability and help meet your manufacturing requirements, contact us at any time! Our network of national sales representatives and local customer service personnel will quickly respond to your needs.

Services

Engineering and Estimating

Beginning with customer blueprints and or design concepts, our experienced staff determines the most efficient way to machine each part. In addition, we assist each customer in finding unique solutions to their machining requirements. In every case, close attention to cost is always observed.

Turning

At the heart of our plant is the primary machining department which consists of 25 automatic screw machines with capacities through 5' 1/2" . Additional capability is found in the form of 8 Bullard Mult-Au-Matics whose 12" swing encompasses most engineering diameters. A variety of other equipment including CNC Lathes, Warner Swasey A.C's, Bore-Matic's and Fuji and Hardinge speed lathes complete the line up.

Broaching

Long a strength of the company, our Broaching Department is equipped with 12 machines and can work internally and externally to the limits of our 66" stroke, 15 ton machines. This means, "we can generally broach anything we can turn."

Grinding

Because most of our work is done to very close tolerances, our Grinding Department is very important to us. We are equipped to I.D., O.D. and Blanchard Grind to very close finish tolerances.

Precision Assemblies

This is where our plant's diverse capabilities are used to their best advantage -- in providing assemblies of finished precision parts. Also, for engineering or economic reasons, in-process components may be assembled and then machined to finish tolerances. In either case, by performing the work, "in-house", we provide "ready to use" components at very competitive prices.
